About this project

What you were just looking at is a piece of fiction, styled as an archive interface from within its own story. Second Nature is a long-form speculative fiction project about a future in which an artificial superintelligence has quietly solved most of humanity's material problems.

The archive framing is intentional: the site presents itself as a database used by people even further in the future than the story's own setting, browsing preserved historical records. Nothing on the main interface is written to explain itself to a present-day visitor, because nothing in the fiction would.

This page, and the privacy page linked below, are the two places on the site written directly to you, the actual person reading this, rather than in the voice of the fiction.

The project is written by its author using an iterative process with AI assistance for prose drafting, with all direction, structure, and editorial decisions belonging to the author.

Privacy & data

This site does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data about visitors.

There are no accounts for readers, no tracking scripts, no analytics services, no advertising cookies, and no third-party trackers. Browsing this site does not create a record anywhere of what you read or when тАФ including the "query" field on the archive interface, which searches only the records already loaded in your browser and sends nothing anywhere.
